CCANB Membership & Chinese School Registration

The online registration for 2012-2013 is available until Sept. 23, 2012.
GENERAL INFORMATION:
CCANB membership will be registered for full year, from September 1st to August 31st of the following year. Membership is valid only when full registration is completed and membership fee is paid.
MEMBERSHIP DUES

Individuals
  1. Regular: $15.00
  2. Seniors (age 65 and older): $10.00
  3. Full-time university/college students: $5.00
Family membership (parents and children)
  1. Regular: $25.00
  2. Parents are full-time university/college students: $10.00
