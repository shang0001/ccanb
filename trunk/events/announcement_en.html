Driver License Translation

The Public Safety Department of New Brunswick had authorized CCANB to do the driver license translation service. The service is free for CCANB registered numbers.

Please follow the process below to obtain:

  1. Go to Chinese Association's Chinese School to drop off your original driver's license.
    Time: every Chinese School day on Sunday.9-10 am
    Location: Chinese School, McCain Hall, STU
  2. You will pick up your translation a week later at the same time and location

This new process takes effect from Sep 15th, 2013.


A congratulatory letter of the Chinese Moon Festival celebration from the MP office

The Chinese Cultural Association of New Brunswick (CCANB) and the Professional Chinese Association of New Brunswick (PCANB) will be holding a Potluck Dinner to celebrate the Moon Festival. It will be held at Odell Park Lodge from 5:30 PM to 8:00 PM on Sunday, Sept. 22, 2013.
