Message to Sponsors:


Dear Friends and Patrons,

2007 marks the 30th anniversary of our Association and Chinese Language School. We are delighted to invite you to join us in celebrating this milestone together, including our special Autumn Moon Dinner Benefit on September 29th, and our grand cultural Gala at the Fredericton Playhouse on October 27th, 2007.

For 30 years, CCANB has made significant contributions to the cultural and educational experiences of the residents of Fredericton and beyond. We have had many successes in the promotion of cross-cultural awareness and inclusiveness within the context of multiculturalism. In anticipation of recent municipal, provincial and regional economic and immigration initiatives, as well as the growing needs of our Association, we are launching our milestone Pearl Fundraising Campaign.

Together, we will make our 30th anniversary theme, "Living in Harmony, in our Maritime Home", a triumphant reality.

Thank you for your sponsorship and support.


Sincerely,

Tom Ng, President
Chinese Cultural Association of NB Doris Wu, Chair
CCANB Pearl Fundraising Campaign
