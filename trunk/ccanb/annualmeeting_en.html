Annual General Meeting

The Annual General Meeting (AGM)of the Chinese Cultural Association of New Brunswick 2013 was held on June 16th, 2013 at the McCain Hall of St. Thomas University.

24 members were in attendance.

The agenda:

  1. Approve minutes from last Annual Meeting
  2. President’s report - Limin Liu
  3. Chinese School Report – Jasmine Murat
  4. Youth Group Report – Richard Du and Amanda Eng
  5. Financial Report – Xiang Yan
  6. Proposal to change to CCANB bylaws made by Limin Liu
  7. Election of new CCANB Executive
