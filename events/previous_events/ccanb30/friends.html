Friends of the Chinese Cultural Association of New Brunswick

Another of CCANB's Tricennial Anniversary projects is the establishment of a special funding vehicle, called the FRIENDS OF CCANB, in response to members' requests and to meet the needs and challenges of New Brunswick's growing Chinese community and newcomer families. Friends of CCANB allows donors to help sustain our association's tradition of excellence by giving generously in the following categories:

Platinum $5,000
Gold $3,000
Silver $2,000
Corporate $1,000
Small Business $500

Funds raised will be directed mainly to CCANB's Building Fund and the names of donors who contribute $500 or more will be inscribed on our Donor Plaque of Honor. (We hope, in time, to make all donations tax deductible.)
